# Getting Started with Nebulae

Welcome to Nebulae! This guide will help you get started with your new privacy-focused social network.

---

## First Login

Your administrator will provide you with:
- **Username**: Usually your email address
- **Password**: Your initial password

### Logging In

1. Navigate to your Nebulae instance (e.g., `https://nebulae.example.com`)
2. Click **Login**
3. Enter your username and password
4. Click **Log In**

![Nebulae login page](/docs/assets/images/login-screen.png)


### Changing Your Password

We recommend changing your password after first login:

1. Click your profile picture (top right)
2. Go to **Settings**

![Nebulae settings icon](/docs/assets/images/settings-icon.png)

3. Navigate to **Account ‚Üí Change Password**
4. Enter your new password
5. Enter your new password (twice)
6. Enter your old password
7. Click **Save Account Settings**

![Nebulae change password](/docs/assets/images/password-change.png)

## Setting Up Your Profile

### Profile Picture

1. Click your profile picture (top left) or the **My Profile** button on the top
2. Click **Upload Profile Picture** to use a photo from your device
3. Or Click **Browse Media** to look for an existing picture
5. If uploading an image (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
6. Adjust cropping if needed
7. Click **Save**

### Display Name

Your display name is what others see throughout Nebulae.

1. Go to your profile
2. Click **Edit** in the **About** section
3. Change your display name
4. Click **Save Changes**

### Profile Information

Add optional information like:
- Hometown
- Bio
- Show your Username (your email address - not recommended)
- Occupation
- Show Friends list
- Birthday visibility
- Pick Family members from your Friends list

All fields are optional and can be made public, local, friends or private (nothing ticked).

## Your First Post

### Creating a Post

1. From your **Home** or **User Profile page**, find the post creation box at the top
2. Type your message (supports basic text)
3. Mention friends with `@username`
4. Add Media (see below)
5. Tag Friends (Optional)
6. Add a location (Optional)
7. Choose privacy setting:
   - üîí **Local** - Only your node
   - üë• **Friends** - Friends on all nodes
   - üåê **Public** - Everyone can see
8. Click **Post**

### Adding Media

1. Click the **Browse Media** or **Upload Media** button
2. Select photos or videos
3. Post as normal
4. Click on the media afterwards to then add user tags and captions.

### Post Privacy Explained

- **Local**: Only users on your Nebulae instance can see it
- **Friends**: Your friends across all connected nodes see it
- **Public**: Visible to everyone, including visitors
- **Group**: Only visible to group members
- **Event**: Only visible to event attendees

## Understanding Your Feed

### How the Feed Works

Nebulae uses a **chronological feed** ‚Äî posts appear in the order they were created. No algorithm, no manipulation, just real-time updates.

### Feed Sources

Your feed includes:
- ‚úÖ Your own posts
- ‚úÖ Posts from friends
- ‚úÖ Posts on your timeline (wall posts)
- ‚úÖ Group posts
- ‚úÖ Event posts
- ‚úÖ Public page updates you follow

### Feed Controls

#### Snooze Friends

Temporarily hide posts from someone:
1. Click the **‚ãØ** menu on any of their posts or via **My Friends**
2. Select **Snooze [name]**
3. User is snooozed for 30 days, or until you Unsnooze - whichever comes first

#### Block Users

For serious issues:
1. Go to the user's profile or **My Friends**
2. Click **‚ãØ** menu
3. Select **Block User**
4. Confirm

Blocked users:
- Can't see your posts
- Can't comment on your content
- Can't tag you

## Interacting with Posts

### Comments

1. Click into the text field on any post
2. Type your comment
3. @mention people if desired
4. Add media to your comment
5. Click **Post Comment**

#### Nested Replies

Reply to specific comments:
1. Click **Reply** under a comment
2. Your reply is indented
3. Conversation threads naturally

### Reposting

Share someone else's public post to your timeline:
1. Click **...** on any public post
2. Click Share
3. Confirm or Cancel the reposting to your timeline.

The original post appears on your timeline with attribution.

## Privacy & Security

### Post Privacy Settings

Always check before posting:
- Default privacy is **Local**
- Can be changed per post
- Can make posts more public after posting
- Can make posts more private


### Two-Factor Authentication (Recommended)

Add extra security to your account:

1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Security**
2. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes in a safe place

‚ö†Ô∏è **Save backup codes!** You'll need them if you lose your phone.

## Media Management

### Uploading Media

#### In Posts/Comments

1. Click **Browse Media** or **Upload Media** when creating a post
2. Select photos/videos
3. Media is automatically processed
4. Thumbnails generated for fast loading

### Browsing Your Media

All media attached to your posts is automatically displayed in your media gallery, as are any media items you are tagged in.
All your uploaded media appears in:
**My Media**

You can:
- View full-size images
- See comments on photos
- Tag friends
- Add a location

#### Albums

Create organized photo albums:
1. Go to **My Media**
2. Click **Albums** tab
3. Select **Create Album**
4. Give the album a title and optional description
5. Click **Create Album**
6. Click into newly created album
7. Click **Add Media** to select media from your media gallery
8. Media items respect the privacy setting of the parent post, meaning 'Local' media will not be seen by friends on other nodes


### Tagging in Photos

Tag friends in photos:
1. Open a photo
2. Click **Tag People**
3. Select friends you want to tag
6. Click **Save**

Tagged friends are notified.
